# E-commerce reporting system

You are building a simple **e-commerce reporting system.** 

There are multiple types of products: `Book`, `DVD`, `Furniture`. Each product has a **`price`**

Your task is to calculate the tax for each product.

1. Books: 10% tax
2. DVDs: 20% tax
3. Furniture: 15% tax

- **bad-code.ts**: The messy version. Do **not** use this one.  
- **better-code.ts**: The clean and proper version. Use this one :)
