### Scenario

Imagine you want to build a House object in your application.

- A simple house: 4 walls, 1 floor, 1 door, 2 windows, 1 roof  
- A luxury house: larger, with a backyard, swimming pool, garage, heating system, electrical wiring, etc.
- Or even other types of home ..

Your goal: **build House objects step by step**, optionally choosing which parts to include
